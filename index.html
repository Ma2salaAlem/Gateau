// About Us

Gâteau is a modern French-inspired bistro and café nestled in the heart of Addis Ababa. We craft each plate and cup with passion — from flaky viennoiseries baked fresh each morning to hand-pulled espresso and seasonal French cuisine. A place to slow down, savour, and feel at home.
